CyConex Cyber Graph Studio is a state-of-the-art cybersecurity risk and compliance management tool that’s helping to reshape the way businesses now approach their cybersecurity, risk and compliance posture.

CyConex’s revolutionary cyber security, risk management, and controls monitoring software offers a powerful solution which integrates advanced graph theory and algorithms – enabling organizations across any scale or sector to both visualize as well as manage their cyber risk and cyber compliance with the utmost ease and insight. It’s the ultimate way to improve your compliance operations and controls monitoring, while also helping to not only reduce security risk but also reduce legal risk.

Key Features of CyConex

Security & Compliance and
Risk Management made easy

Graph-based Visualization

CyConex uses an interactive and intuitive graph system at its core to depict your current cybersecurity and overall cyber risk landscape. View all your assets and their interconnectedness, as well as their associated vulnerabilities and potential attack points. All of this is presented in an easy-to-understand and straightforward layout, requiring no technical training at all.

Simultaneously manage Cyber Risk and Compliance

Use the same graph-based visualization to simultaneously model your cyber risk and framework compliance, allowing you to focus your compliance activities and controls monitoring on what matters the most.

Integration & Scalability

CyberGuard Graphix was built to seamlessly integrate with your existing infrastructure. This includes vulnerability scanners, SIEM systems, and a plethora of IT management tools. Our cyber compliance and cyber security software is highly scalable to meet the growing demands of organizations of all scales, no matter what their sector.

Cyber Risk Management

Using CyConex on a day-to-day basis can help you visualize specific relationships with unparalleled ease – such as relationships between your organizational assets and how specific threat actors, attacks and vulnerabilities can exploit them. This allows you to deeply understand the risks which can propagate throughout your organisation, by providing a clear and transparent view of your full risk profile or landscape.

Predictive analytics and AI-driven insights

CyConex is powered by advanced machine learning (ML) algorithms, with its dedicated CyberGuard Graphix module being capable of not only assessing current risks but also predicting potential future threats with unmatched accuracy. This advanced but very easy-to-use predictive analytics feature aids in proactive risk management rather than reactive cyber risk management, thereby significantly cutting down the likelihood of cyber incidents.

Compliance Assessment & Management

CyConex comes integrated with a comprehensive compliance module, ensuring that you always adhere to the latest cyber regulations and standards, including CCPA, GDPR, NIST, and ISO 27001. Our compliance management and cyber risk management software automatically maps these compliance standards on the graph, allowing you to easily spot any possible issues or shortcomings.

Advanced Reporting & Dashboards

Detailed controls monitoring and cyber risk reports as well as custom dashboards offer a quick overview of your current security stance, your compliance status, and identified risks. These reports are very easy to understand and digest, with the added benefit of customization according to your ongoing needs.
